Dr Udatta Kher, B.D.S. (1990) M.D.S.(1995)
Spl attn: Dental Implants, Oral Surgery, Cosmetic Dentistry


Dr. Udatta Kher graduated from Government Dental College and Hospital, Mumbai in 1990 and followed that up with masters in Oral Surgery from the same institute. He maintains two practices at Pali Hill and Lokhandwala complex, Mumbai. His main focus in practice is towards implantology, laser dentistry and restorative dentistry. He is a visiting Professor in the 'Implantology Department' at SDM Dental College, Dharwad. He is the 'Study Club Director' for the Mumbai Seacoast Study Club of the 'International Team for Implantology'. He is also a registered 'ITI Speaker'. He is a Founder member of the 'Indian Academy of Laser Dentistry'. Dr. Kher also holds a 'Diploma in Laser Dentistry' from Aachen University, Germany. He is the Director of 'Impart Education', an academy for guiding and nurturing clinicians towards evidence based dental practice. He is also an expert on Dental XP, one of the largest portals for online education in the world.

Dr Sudha Shenoy, B.D.S.(1991)
Spl attn: Restorative dentistry, Endodontics, Removable Dentures


Dr Sudha Shenoy, BDS, is a consultant Dental Surgeon at Only Smiles Dental Centre. Having graduated from the prestigious Government Dental College and Hospital, Mumbai in 1991, followed by Clinical Assistantship in the same hospital. She has more than, 23 years of experience in the field of Dentistry.

For Dr Sudha, dentistry is as much as an art as a science. With her congenial, intuitive and caring nature, she's able to treat the young and the old with equal ease.

Her areas of interest and expertise lie in treating geriatric patients requiring removable prosthesis (dentures). She also takes pride in doing painless, single sitting root canal treatments. She has a keen eye for detail and that helps her in offering the best possible treatment plan to her patients.

She has been practicing at the current position since 2004 and her practice has grown steadily, thanks to the referrals from her satisfied, happy patients.

Dr Sudha has a passion for the performing arts as well as the fine arts. She enjoys doing charcoals and wall murals. She is a member of a popular, contemporary choir which has performed at many prestigious venues.

She also has a lovely supportive family, which applauds her as she achieves her goals!

Dr Vidya Nath B.D.S.(1992) M.D.S.(1995) MSc.(Lon)
Spl attn: Prosthodontics, Restorative Dentistry, TMJ disorders


Dr.Vidya D Nath, who has been an associate with OSDC for the last 7 years, completed her basic degree in Dentistry at Nair Hospital Dental College in Mumbai and secured entry to the MDS program in Prosthodontics at the same institute. She finished her post graduate studies in 1994 and left to pursue work opportunities in the UK. She worked in private and hospital-based referral practice in London for 11 years. She secured a coveted seat in the UK's premier Eastman Dental Institute to pursue a second full-time Master's degree in Restorative Dentistry and Implantology in 2005. She was ranked first in the course with a 'Double' first class. She consolidated her training by joining as a staff member of the Restorative team at the Eastman and had a chance to work with world class clinicians there. She was also a trainer and examiner for foreign trained dentists seeking to work in the UK.

Dr Meenakshi Kher, B.D.S.(1991) M.D.S.(1995)
Spl attn: Pediatric Dentistry


Dr. Meenakshi Kher, M.D.S. (Pedodontics), has pioneered the field of Pediatric Dental practice in India. She received her Masters degree in Pediatric Dentistry from the University of Mumbai in 1995 and established the 'HAPPY TOOTH CLINIC'. The practice, one of the first of its kind in the country... is dedicated exclusively to the dental needs of children.

Over the years, Dr. Meenakshi's passion and commitment to treating children has ensured that several thousands of children have successfully received quality dental care at the centre. She takes a special interest in treating children who are differently abled and with special needs.

Dr. Meenakshi has been keenly involved in continuing dental education and is one the most sought after speakers in her field. She lectures extensively, conducting courses and workshops at various national forums. Her workshop, 'Pedopathshala' has gained immense popularity amongst general dental practitioners and pediatric dentists alike.

Dr. Meenakshi's expertise is also well acknowledged internationally. In 2007 she was invited by the prestigious International Association of Pediatric Dentistry to make a presentation about her exclusive children's practice at the 21st IAPD International Congress, held in Hong Kong.

Dr. Meenakshi loves travelling and trekking with husband Udatta and their son Saatvik. She is passionate about Indian classical music and makes time in her busy schedule for the continuation of her study in vocal music.
